About TalentXid

Empowering Indonesian Migrant Workers

Vision & Mission

Empowering Indonesian Workers for Global Opportunities with TalentXid

Our vision is to revolutionize the process of connecting Indonesian workers with overseas employment opportunities by providing comprehensive support from job search to post-arrival assistance, ensuring compliance, quality, and trust.

Our mission is to offer seamless support for Indonesian talents throughout their overseas employment journey, ensuring regulatory compliance, safety, and personalized assistance. We aim to foster productive working relationships by understanding the unique cultural and professional synergies between Indonesia and other countries.
